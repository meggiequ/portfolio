SF Mural Artist Tracking App

Role: UX UI Design, App Development | Duration: 3 months

OVERVIEW

THE PROBLEM

San Francisco is the hub for mural artists where they use city walls as their canvas to create and inspire. Many artists have fans across the world who follow their works and visit their murals in the city. Murals also has become a hot theme on social media. However it is very hard to track artists’ murals throughout the town.

THE SOLUTION

A plarform where you can track your favourite artists’ murals in the city. You can add photos and the location of a mural you found and find photos and location of a particular mural you want to visit.
